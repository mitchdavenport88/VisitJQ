About the JQ

The Jewellery Quarter is steeped in heritage and teaming with both traditional and modern independent businesses. This unique neighbourhood has a village atmosphere and offers the chance to explore Birmingham's history, learn a new skill or enjoy award-winning cafes and restaurants. Plan your visit today!

From the city centre, the Quarter is just a 20-minute walk. We also have a dedicated train station situated on Vyse Street. West Midland Metro services also operate at frequent intervals. A one-way trip from New Street station is a 7-minute journey.

Eat

The JQ food and drink scene has flourished in recent years and has seen an influx in many fine dining restaurants, many of which are vying for their Michelin star. This has meant that the JQ is fast becoming a mecca for foodies and cocktail lovers.

Whether your thing is street food, pub grub or fine dining; with a wide range of cuisines and price points there is sure to be something for everyone to enjoy. See our interactive map below for our recommendations!

Drink

Swanky bars, micro-breweries and traditional pubs. The Jewellery Quarter can offer a nightlife that’s a refreshing alternative to the busy Birmingham city centre. Home to some of Birmingham’s best beer gardens and rooftop terraces perfect for those summer days!

Two local breweries; Rock & Roll Brewhouse and Burning Soul Brewery operate out of the area. If real Ale is more your thing we also host the infamous JQ Ale Trail that visitors the chance to explore the large range of pubs on offer.

Explore & Discover

The JQ contains Europe’s largest concentration of manufacturing jewellers but the Jewellery Quarter is more than just its namesake. Steeped in a rich history and is a true wonder to explore. Delve deeper at local museums and visitor attractions or take a guided tour of the area.

With over 200 listed buildings, including a Georgian church and Victorian Cemeteries, much of the Jewellery Quarter’s heritage can be explored on foot. The interactive map below highlights interests across the area for you to explore at your leisure.
